## Order Strategy

### Reject an order when:
- Distance is too long
- Pay is too low for the effort
- Long wait at the restaurant (slow kitchens that waste time)
- Delivery goes to a far area with no orders back to base

### Accept an order when:
- Reasonable distance, fair pay
- Fast pickup
- Delivery stays in or near the home area (Nes Ziona preferred)
