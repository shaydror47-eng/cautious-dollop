## Common Issues & Quick Fixes

| Issue | Symptom | Fix | Prevent |
|-------|---------|-----|----------|
| App won't start | Crash on launch | Force stop > Clear cache > Restart | Keep app updated |
| GPS not working | Waze can't navigate | Restart location services | Keep location ON always |
| Phone overheats | Device too hot | Stop work, switch phone, let cool | Monitor app usage |
| Storage full | Apps slow/crash | Delete cache or old files | Check weekly |
| No notifications | Wolt orders silent | Check app notifications enabled | Verify in Settings |
| Battery drain | Drains in 2-3 hours | Find heavy app in Settings > Battery | Disable background apps |
| WiFi/Data drops | No mobile signal | Restart phone, toggle airplane mode | Check signal strength |
| Button not working | App won't respond | Force stop app, try again | Test daily |
