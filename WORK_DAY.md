# Work Day

## Purpose
Daily shift routine for Wolt delivery work: when to work, phone rules, and what to record after every shift.

## Standard Shift
- **Main shift**: Evening 18:00–22:00 (best orders and money)
- **Main area**: Nes Ziona; secondary: Rehovot, Rishon LeZion, Yavne, Beer Yaakov
- **Weekly earnings target**: ₪3,000+ (details in WOLT.md)

## Before Shift
1. Charge both phones to 80%+
2. Quick check: Wolt, Waze/GPS, notifications on both phones (checklist in PHONE.md)
3. Take charger/power bank

## During Shift
- Work on Phone 1 (Galaxy S23); Phone 2 is backup
- If a phone fails — switch immediately, max 2 minutes of troubleshooting
- Reject orders per the rules in WOLT.md (too far, low pay, long restaurant wait, far destination with no return)

## After Shift
1. Write a short entry in DAILY_LOG.md (see template below)
2. Any problem during the shift → BUGS.md
3. Phone status changed → update PHONE.md

## Daily Entry Template (copy into DAILY_LOG.md)
```
Date:
Shift hours:
Earnings: ₪
Orders: accepted / rejected
Areas worked:
Phone issues: none /
Notes for next shift:
```
